# **Social Proof Widget**

**Show your users that your website is active, trusted, and converting — effortlessly.**

`social-proof-widget` is an open-source React and vanilla JS widget that displays real-time social proof notifications on your website. Perfect for e-commerce, SaaS, and service sites, it helps increase trust, engagement, and conversions by showing:

* **Recent Activity:** Notify visitors about recent signups or purchases.
* **Live Visitor Count:** Show how many people are browsing your pages in real time.
* **Hot Streaks:** Highlight trending products or popular pages.
* **Custom Messaging & Triggers:** Tailor notifications for maximum effect.

**Why use it?**

* **Easy to install:** Just `npm install @yourorg/social-proof-widget` and embed.
* **Open source:** Customize and extend to fit your site’s design.
* **Privacy-first:** Data is encrypted end-to-end; you never expose sensitive user info.
* **Scalable:** Works on small sites or large platforms without slowing your page.
